# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful in discussions and reviews.
- Assume good intent and ask clarifying questions.
- Give constructive, actionable feedback.
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Personal attacks, trolling, or inflammatory comments.
- Sharing private information without explicit permission.
- Any conduct that is inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Scope

This policy applies within project spaces and in public spaces when representing the project, including:

- GitHub issues
- Pull requests
- Discussions
- Linked social posts related to this repository

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this Code of Conduct and may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, wiki edits, issues, and other contributions that violate this policy.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, contact: `contact@sahanpramuditha.com`

All reports will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
